Digital Elevation Models (DEMs)

Digital Elevation Models are models that can represent Earth's surface in different ways. In 2022, I worked on a solo project that took custom data—in the form of a .tif file—and calculated the most efficient off-trail route between two points in a wilderness setting. The repo can be viewed here.
